Figs. 12–14 are the pressure-derivative plots for different PFO tests performed on Inj-1. For ease of analysis, we consider different intervals of injection volume (time). A close examination would reveal that the responses differ over time. More important, there are clear indications of fracturing and damage. From these responses, well, reservoir and fracture characteristics have been established, with relevant results summarised in Table 1. To understand the range of uncertainties in fracture length, the dynamic-fracture model was used to re-analyse the two periods characterised by either half-unit or quarter-unit slope, thereby providing a basis to compare the static and dynamic fracture models.
