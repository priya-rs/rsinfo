After the first stage of separation (Psep 106 bar, Tsep - 30°C) high pressure gas will be headed to the GTP under its own pressure for joint treatment with reservoir gas. Separated oil, condensate and water are headed to the second separation stage (Psep 20 bar, Tsep - 25°C) after which they are supplied to the OTP for further oil treatment and release into the export transport system.
