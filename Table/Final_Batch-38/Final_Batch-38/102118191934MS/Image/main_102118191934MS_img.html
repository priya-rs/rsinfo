Junggar Basin is located in northwest China (Figure 1a), Jimusaer Sag is located in the eastern uplift of Junggar Basin, Jimusaer County, Xinjiang (Figure 1b). The whole is a long-term negative sedimentary unit and formed at the end of Carboniferous. It is characterized by a dustpan depression with low-lying east to the west, area is 1278 km2. Jimusaer Sag experienced the influence of late Hercynian, Indosinian, Yanshan and Himalayan tectonic movements. The scale strength and influence of different tectonic movements are different. The Lucaogou Formation of the Permian is distributed in the whole sag with a thickness of 200-350 m (Figure 1c).

Compared with Figure 2, the data points in Figure 5 are concentrated near the diagonal line, and the correlation is very good, which shows that the corrected static brittleness index is in good agreement with the experimental results and the reliability of the model is good.

The laboratory rock mechanical experiment is carried out by the rock triaxial stress apparatus. Due to the difference of composition and structure, the deformation mechanism of rock is complicated. The stress-strain curve of rock can be classified into three types (Figure 6). Low porosity sandstone and limestone are typical brittle rocks, which are considered to be better in petroleum engineering and linear in stress-strain curve, as shown in Figure 6(a). The failure occurs before the large nonlinear deformation, the mode is brittle failure, it is the occurrence and development of micro-fracture.

Based on the rock mechanics characteristics and fracture morphology, the 85 experimental results in the study area are divided into three types: good brittleness, medium brittleness and poor brittleness, drawing static brittleness index and Poisson's ratio cross plot, as shown in Figure 7, When the static brittleness index is more than 85 and Poisson's ratio is less than 0.2, the reservoir shows good brittleness, when the static brittleness index is less than 40 and Poisson's ratio is greater than 0.24, the reservoir shows poor brittleness. When the static brittleness index and Poisson's ratio are between them, the reservoir shows moderate brittleness.
