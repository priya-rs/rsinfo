A total of 19 rock samples were measured various petrophysical and rock mechanical parameters, including helium porosity, clay content, longitudinal wave velocity, shear wave velocity, static Young's modulus and static Poisson's ratio. In order to avoid the influence on the experimental results, rock samples are well cemented, without cracks, the confining pressure and temperature of Young's modulus and Poisson's ratio are measured in a similar range by the triaxial stress experimental system of rock mechanics.
