A summary on increasing capillary number to reduce residual fluid saturation is presented in Figure 1. This summary is resumed from various phenomena of fluid flow in porous media, which covers oil, microemulsion and brine flow. Figure 1 portrays the attempt to recover more fluid either oil or brine is achievable by increasing trapping number NT. The contribution of trapping number can be approached either by capillary number side or Bond number side. Bond number covers the density difference Δγ, fluid bubble size d and both wettability θ and surface tension Ts.
