Three of the remaining tests were examining more of a flood type response where increased production was expected at wells offset from the injector. Two of these pilots were water injection projects, and the third one was an enriched natural gas injection project that followed one of the water injection cases. The other test was a CO2 injection pilot where the injection occurred into a vertical well that was near a horizontal well.
