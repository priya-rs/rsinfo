To test this approach, we simulated the production rates from a 5 × 4 homogeneous model with high permeability (Table 3) and BHPs constant employing a commercial simulator. The simulation is run for 299 months and the data are sampled monthly (Figure 4 and Figure 5). When we apply the CM to evaluate IWC for this case, we deliberately exclude I05, so we create an imbalance in the system before running the CM.

To illustrate this, we use the same 5 × 4 semi-homogeneous model for testing used in Case 2. For this case, P01 has a shut-in time of 15 months, and the CMP-PW predicted production rates better match the simulated rates than the comseg-CM (Table 5). The CMP-PW results are less affected by the long shut-in (Figure 23). In particular, the comseg-CM estimated λs with shut-in underestimate the long-distance well pair λ’s (e.g., I01-P03).
