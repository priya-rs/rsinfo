In evaluating the initial in-place uncertainty in an OUT and ODT scenario, we carried out sensitivity on the possible fluid contact variations (ODT, Mid Point, WUT and Spill Point). For the OUT we used pressure and GOR information to draw conclusion that the reservoir is undersaturated and thus we filled the column with oil to the crest.
