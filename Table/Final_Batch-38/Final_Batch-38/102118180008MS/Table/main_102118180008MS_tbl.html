Where modeling concludes that single hop wireless communications are not feasible, then a relay station(s) can be installed in the well at a depth intermediate to the downhole gauge and the subsea receiver. The relay station serves to boost the downhole gauge signal for onward transmission to the subsea receiver. Where conditions are particularly challenging then, whilst highly unusual, it may be necessary to install more than one relay station in the well.
