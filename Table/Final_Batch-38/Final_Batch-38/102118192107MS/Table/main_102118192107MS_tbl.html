By the cementing program data, the thickening time test was stopped at the moment when the displacement plug should be bumped. The cylinder was disassembled, and the rheological parameters of the cement slurry were verified to perform the computer simulation again and identify final pressure with changed slurry rheology (Table 1). The simulation results showed the same high final pressure as the actual on the performed job, where high pressure was identified. This is crucial when using hydraulic casing equipment because during this operation, any deviations from the pumping pressure can lead to unsuccessful results of the job.
