Fig. 5 shows the depth-based PDC bit torque in salt, along with the first hybrid run. All bit runs had a concentric reamer in the BHA, except Run-1. Because of the shearing action of the PDCs, the torque fluctuations are high, even when the reamer was closed. Tab. 1 indicates a high standard deviation or torque fluctuation for PDC bits. High torque fluctuations have the potential for initiating torsional oscillations which can cause the loss of drilling efficiency.
