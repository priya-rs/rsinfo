Based on the typical thick heavy oil reservoir from Bohai offshore oilfield, China, we established a numerical model, as shown in Figure 2. The basic physical parameters of this model are shown in Table 1. The horizontal injector was placed 6 m above the producer. The water-oil relative permeability curve and liquid-gas relative permeability curve are respectively shown in Figure 3.

SAGD process is composed by startup phase (preheating phase), pressure-decline phase and drainage phase. Preheating phase aims to create thermal interconnection between producer well and injector well. There are three approaches, including cyclic steam stimulation, steam circulation and fracturing (Liu 1998; Dong et al. 2014). In this model, the CSS preheating method is adopted. Figure 4 shows the temperature profile after CSS preheating process. As shown, after preheating stage, the temperature between the two horizontal wells has rised to 150°above. Furthermore, the subcool temperature control is also important for SAGD simulation. The value of steam trap we used in this model is 15°.
