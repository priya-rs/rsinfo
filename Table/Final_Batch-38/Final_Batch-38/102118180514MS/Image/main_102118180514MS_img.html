In 6” section, 4 1/2" perforated liner is used to complete 800~1000m long horizontal section to prevent well bore collapsing, the typical casing program is listed in the Figure 1.

Based on reservoir study on N.A oilfield, the cluster well pads and main/access roads will be determined and designed by considering the local marsh area and lots of landmines left during the Iran-Iraq war (please see figure 2). Three dimensional well trajectory will be hired due to parallel spacing deployment of horizontal wells and high deviated wells in cluster wells. 21 well pads are designed based on the well deployment and landscape of N.A oilfield and cluster wells will be drilled on 15 well pads.

21 well pads are designed based on the well deployment and landscape of N.A oilfield and cluster wells will be drilled on 15 well pads. The wells number varies from 2 to 6 horizontal or high deviated wells in each well pad and four or five wells will be drilled in most well pads. The maximum well number is 6 in pad 9 in which one vertical well, four high deviated wells and one horizontal well will be drilled. Well Pad Layout and Cluster Wells Deployment please see Figure 3.

Well pad will be constructed by filled with stones and earth in the swamp and wet land with 1.5-3.5m depth water after cutting reed that causes long construction period and high cost for well pad construction. The average construction period for one pad is about 4-5 months and the cost will amount to 4 million dollars. In order to shorten the well pad construction period and cut the construction cost, cluster wells mode becomes the top option. The well slot distance is determined by 9m and the distance between backup slot and slot is 3m to save the well pad construction area (please see figure 4).

Drop off well inclination drilling shall be begun till 0°degree inclination (about 850-900m well depth) after the anti-collision distance reaches the safety space. The detail directional trajectory design and anti-collision design please see table 2 and figure 5-6. The anti-collision design not only satisfies the demand of well bore anti-collision but also avoiding building up drilling in Gachsaran formation with the potential complicated problems such as high pressure salt water kick and losses.
