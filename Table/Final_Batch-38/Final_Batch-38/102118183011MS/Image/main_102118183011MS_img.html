Complete analysis was conducted based on the subsurface parameters (Figure 7) to ensure any parameter is neither neglected nor given high weight when move forward for detailed history matching phase. Sensitivity analysis (OVAT) showed 5 uncertainty parameters with major impact while other parameters have minor impact. Figure 9 presents the results from the sensitivity study in tornado charts with descending parameters based on their influence on the cumulative oil. Based on uncertainty analysis, permeability (horizontal permeability between injector to producer) and relative permeability have the greatest impact on cumulative oil production (Global) while fault transmissibility has the least effect.
