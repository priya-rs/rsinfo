The AHM starts with the creation of uncertainty space by defining the parameters and their ranges in form of a “variability model”. A set of 10 variability models (Table 1) has been constructed. This initial list of parameters is selected to address the different aspects of the problem statement, for instance, de-coupling of permeability into two components to address the uncertainties in permeability magnitude and contrast (vertical heterogeneity) of the field; Kv/Kh, baffles distribution and aquifer properties to address the issues of water coning, water breakthrough time and fluid flow paths (streamlines); and skin to account for the well deliverability (near wellbore aspects).
