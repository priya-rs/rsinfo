Recent improvements in digital technology (big data implementations) data gathering and management techniques has fostered an important opportunity for the E&P industry. Through the appropriate implementation of knowledge-driven workflows, significant improvements in operational performance and cost efficiencies can be achieved by taking advantage of the big data revolution. Figure 1(a) presents a conceptual evolution of turning raw data in to actionable intelligence utilizing data analytical methods (Holdaway 2014).

Keith Holdaway . 2014. Harness Oil and Gas Big Data with Analytics: Optimize Exploration and Production with Data Driven Models, John Wiley & Sons (Reprint).

Figure 4 is a histogram of various predictor and response parameter (predictors were chosen at random for this toy example) for 522 secondary recovery projects. Secondary to Primary ratios (globally across the Permian Basin) have a median ratio of ~ 0.9; projects with S:P ratios greater than 2 should be evaluated to determine if these projects are outliers (or bad data) or potentially apart of a separate population (e.g. major fields/leases) - for this analysis we have chosen to keep these values in our analysis. Figures 5 and 6 are the summary outputs from our multivariate linear regression analysis (S:P = API Gravity + Injection Well /Production Well Ratio + Injection Pressure Gradient). We observe that while most of the predictor variables are significant (95% confindense level) as well as the overall model; residual standard error is quite high, which will lead to a large confindence band for the response.

The model outline above was a global model (San Andres reservoirs are located accross the entire Permian Basin); we anticipate spatial regression techniques will minimize the residual error, given Walters Law corollary - things tend to be near like things, providing models that are more robust. Figure 7 is provides a pictorial of how spatial distances can act as a weighting (e.g. closer projects have more weight) parameter to our regression model.
