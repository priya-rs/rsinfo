After the natural flow period, the well is transitioned to artificial lift according to the screening process; this comprises the second case or transitional artificial lift stage. As production decline continues during the life of the well, a third stage is approached when the late stage artificial lift system is installed for the remaining production life of the well. This study analyzes each stage and provides recommendations on the artificial lift system for each production stage. Table 1 summarizes the results of the workflow analysis for each case.
