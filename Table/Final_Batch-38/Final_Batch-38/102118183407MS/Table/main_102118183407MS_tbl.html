In order to be able to reproduce the results of PVT experiments, two regression types (plus and characterized fluid regression) were used in the scope of the study. Plus fluid regression is performed while characterizing the fluid thereby treating the coefficients in the property correlations for Tc, Pc, and acentric factor of the C7+ fractions as regression parameters as described by Christensen [2]. Characterized fluid regression was performed after plus fluid regression. Critical pressure and temperature, acentric factor were used as regression parameters. While only C7+ properties were adjusted with the plus fluid regression, all components may have properties adjusted in a characterized fluid regression. All lumped heavy fractions were grouped on this regression stage in order to keep monotonic character of weights and properties (table 4).
