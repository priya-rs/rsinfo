A report for American Petroleum Institute states that ‘basically, any kind of oil reservoir that has been water flooded with success is a good candidate for CO2 EOR″. The reservoir rock could be sandstone or carbonate with high residual oil saturation, however for EOR using CO2to be successful, there are certain rock and fluid property requirements. These requirements constitute the screening criteria for EOR using CO2and they are listed on Table 1. The screening criteria were taken from two sources; Report for American Petroleum Institute, and US Department of Energy. Generally the API gravity of the crude should be greater than 22° while the oil viscosity should be less than 10cp and the preferred oil composition should be high in C5 to C12.
