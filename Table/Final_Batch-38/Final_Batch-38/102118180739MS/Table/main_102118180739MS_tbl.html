As discussed by Moe Soe Let et al. (Moe Soe Let, Manichand and Seright 2012) a viscosity of 40–45 cp was initially injected in the pilot. However subsequent analysis suggested that a better sweep efficiency could be achieved by increasing the injected polymer viscosity thus viscosity was first increased to 85 cp (in November-December 2011) then to 125 cp in February 2013 (Figure 7). In total 0.75 PV of polymer solution were injected in pattern 1M101, 0.64 PV in pattern 1N062 and 0.56 PV in pattern 1M052 (Table 2).
