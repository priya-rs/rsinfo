But as shown in Table1, at the end of the stimulation cycle, more than half of the injected gas are remained underground which also contribute to the gas movement and channeling to the adjacent wells.

Combined with performance parameters of current generator and optimization results, the injection should be decreased in order to decline the possibility of gas breakthrough and improve the development effect. It is suggested that steam injection per unit length should be controlled below 5 m3/m. The specific result shows as Table.2.
