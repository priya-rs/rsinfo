Choosing the right mode of transport is important for an EOR project as the project develops (and the volume dramatically increases) from a single-well test (SWT) to multi-well pilot (MWP) to a full-scale project. For example, surfactant quantity at 100% AM concentration increases from ~1 ton (SWT) to ~1,000 ton (MWP) to 10,000s of ton per year (full scale project). Table 2 summarises the different volume units and transport methods for chemical products, including surfactant concentrates.
