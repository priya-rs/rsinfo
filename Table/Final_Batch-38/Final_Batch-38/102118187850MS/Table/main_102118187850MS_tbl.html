In the sedimentation point of view, the reservoir was formed in deep-water conditions by the deep-marine fan depositions. The total area of the formed fan system is 182 704 thousand square meters. From the lateral differentiation point of view, submarine fan was divided into three main regions: the distal part, the middle and proximal parts. For each of these zones, the distribution of the effective thicknesses was evaluated. The assessment is based on the collection of data from neighboring deposits with have much more collected data (Table 1).
