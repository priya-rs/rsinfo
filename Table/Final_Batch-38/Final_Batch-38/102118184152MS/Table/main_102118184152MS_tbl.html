From Table 5 we can see that both of WZ-OS surfactant and GM-50surfactant are effective in foaming and acceptable as clean-up additives. As a kind of non-Newtonian fluid, the foam is featured with shearing and thinning abilities, while its apparent viscosity in high-permeability formation is higher than that in low-permeability one. Therefore, foams may be used to plug highly permeable formation to with higher a swept volume and sweep efficiency.
