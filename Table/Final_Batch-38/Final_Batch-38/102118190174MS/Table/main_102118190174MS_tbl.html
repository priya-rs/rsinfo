Schrader Bluff produced water samples were used for all experiments. The Schrader Bluff produced water composition is a mix of injected sea water and produced water. Steps were taken to ensure water samples used are representative. Water samples were collected according to a BP preset procedure to ensure no vital information was lost. For instance, pH and iron content were measured on site. Iron content in water influences polymer stability. Water was analyzed for chemical composition, total dissolved solids, viscosity and density (Table 2).
