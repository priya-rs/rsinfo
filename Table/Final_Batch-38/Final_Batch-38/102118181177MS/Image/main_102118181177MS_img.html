The reservoir where the study was conducted is comprised of channel sandstones with interbedded clays of different facies; this is in structural traps, where a high compartmentalization is observed, with proven hydrocarbon accumulations. The study area is located in western Venezuela, Trujillo state, the Ceiba field, as shown in Fig. 1.
