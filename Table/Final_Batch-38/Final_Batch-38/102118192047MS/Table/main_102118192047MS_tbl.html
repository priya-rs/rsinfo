The attractive outcomes of the pilot HFA campaign consecutively speeded up the fully-implemented HFB campaign. Hence, all the newly drilled and existing wells were reviewed for potential candidates. The main basic criteria were developed based on internal discussions, expert recommendations, literature reviews, and brainstorming sessions, see Table 1. For new well candidates, the subsurface criteria is the main focus. Nevertheless, current production, well integrity, and future plan also considered for existing wells. General types of existing wells, which are interesting, are: uneconomic wells, observation wells, and low production wells.
