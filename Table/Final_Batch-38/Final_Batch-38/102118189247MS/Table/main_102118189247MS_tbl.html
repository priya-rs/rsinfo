On the basis of relevant physical modeling and numerical simulation (No details presented in this paper), a new polymer flooding assembly slug was designed. It was injected with produced water through pre-set slug+ main slug as shown in Table 2, with accumulated injection amount of 0.06PV (27.8×104 m3), and injection period of 463 days. The injection program for single wells is shown in Fig.3. According to the interlayer residual oil distribution and injection-production correlation, mechanical sealing and unsealing treatments were taken for a part of injectors and producers, and re-perforation treatments were taken on one injector and one producer.
