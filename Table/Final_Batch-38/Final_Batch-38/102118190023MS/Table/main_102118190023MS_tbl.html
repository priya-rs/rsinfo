Table 1 summarizes the typical parameters utilized in the geomechanics model, which were collected from field work. Stage 2 was the target candidate for fracturing in the planned restimulation. Prior to field execution, the coupled geomechanical and reservoir model was used to predict the re-fracturing performance and identify potential risks by varying diverter designs. During refracturing, all the pre-defined clusters with well propagated fractures were opened simultaneously for fracturing fluid flow.
