The pilot saw first steam on November 15, 2015. A summary of key dates and events is shown in Table 1. Steam circulation start-up was performed for approximately one and half months and was followed by production mode using an ESP in the producer. Injector downhole pressure was maintained at approximately 2,500 kPa.

A summary of the reservoir model input parameters used in the SAP history match are shown in Table 2. Solvent properties like viscosity and equilibrium k-values are studied in the lab. To take account into the measurement error, the solvent viscosity and k-values are allowed to change slightly (0.8 – 1.2 times variance from lab data) in the history match study. The steam rate varied from 0.8 to 1.2 times of field steam production rate for the SAP history match to account for the measurement error.
