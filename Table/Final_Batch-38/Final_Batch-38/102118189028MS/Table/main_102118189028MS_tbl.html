The analysis of the results obtained was conducted (Table 1). As can be seen from Table 1 and analysis of the well injection capacity profiles, the injection capacity of wells No. 942, 1184, and 1246 increased (Fig. 1a) and injection capacity of wells No. 1299 and 1410 decreased (Fig. 1b). The increase in the injection capacity of three wells is connected with the presence of carbonate rocks in the well bottom zone of the formation. Thus, the injection capacity of well No. 1184, where rock carbonation is 12.7%, increased more than 6 times. There are no carbonate rocks in the rocks of the well bottom zone of wells No. 1299 and 1410.

After the measures had been carried out, the performance of the production wells (19 wells) in the deposit area in question changed (Table 2).
