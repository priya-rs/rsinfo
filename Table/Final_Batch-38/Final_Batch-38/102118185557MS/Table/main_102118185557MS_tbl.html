To regionalize Eaton's pore-pressure model, the convergence analysis approach was applied, as it was explained before; however, alpha exponent (α) from Eaton equation was a great unknow until it was evaluated with the resistivity and sonic travel time data from several wells drilled at Mexican Gulf Coast. The results revealed that the alpha exponent is smaller than its original value for both logs; results are showed in table 1.
