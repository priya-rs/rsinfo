The trial well candidate was re-opened, tested and PLT’d in 2015 to establish a base-line for current conditions and for designing the ICD completion. The average ratios were: 0.4% oil cut (OC), 99.6% water cut (WC), 32,000 scf/bbl gas oil ratio (GOR), with rates up to 2,050 blpd, 10 bopd and 346 Mscfd, as shown in Table 2 and Figure 22.

In addition, PLT was performed over three days in August 2015. Several passes were executed using Dual DEFT tool, followed by additional run with DEFT/GHOST PLT tools. Table 8 provides a summary of the field data collected to date for the three configurations tested so far. Clearly there is a significant benefit for the field under EOR schemes from conventional ICDs and AICVs. However, because of the leak discovered in the plug below M3 zone, which is believed occurred either after the first test was completed or the second test was completed, we cannot draw any firm conclusion on the much higher expected performance of the AICVs.
