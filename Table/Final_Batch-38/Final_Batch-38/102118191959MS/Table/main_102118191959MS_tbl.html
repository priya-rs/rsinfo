Table 1 Advantages and disadvantages of deterministic modeling

Deterministic Modeling (Most likely single realization)
Advantages Disadvantages
• Simpler and faster.
• Adequate manpower and software exist to carry out the study.
• Expertise are available.		 • Uncertainty on the P50 model itself, where ‘P50 may not be the real Most Likely’.
• Single deterministic value of reserves and production profile. L,M,H are scenario based, not associated to probability.
• Scenarios may not be representative and do not include uncertainties in “true” sense.
• Range of real solution i.e. recovery and production profile of real P90-P50-P10 cases are not known at the time of decision making
• Interactions of various uncertainties are not captured. Tornado chart expresses impact of sensitivity only, (changing variable one at a time).
• No proper identification of key uncertainties and risks, therefore affecting risks mitigation and data acquisition plan.

Table 1 and 2 compare the advantages and disadvantages of deterministic vs probabilistic approaches.

Table 2 Advantages and disadvantages of deterministic modeling

Table 2 Advantages and disadvantages of deterministic modeling

Probabilistic Modeling (Multi-realizations L/M/H)
Advantages Disadvantages
• More representative P90-P50-P10 models by incorporating static and dynamic uncertainties from beginning (not deterministic or scenario based forecasts).
• Provides real probabilistic range of forecasts and reserves (P90-P50-P10), therefore better project definition and decision making.
• Interactions and dependencies between uncertainties are captured.
• More robust analysis on estimating impact of uncertainties on forecasts and reserves (Risk).
• More focused strategies for risks mitigation and further data acquisition.
• Auditable process.		 • The outcomes of probabilistic workflow will depend upon the understanding and definition of uncertainties and their ranges.
• Uncertainties in defining the ranges can dominate the results.
• Result can also be biased/skewed, e.g. if parameter with P10-P90 ranges are mixed with P40-P60.
• Proxy validation doesn’t prove that the proxy is predictive, but only confirm that it was adequate to match the data used in the analysis.
• It is as correct as the selection and Description of the key uncertainties.
