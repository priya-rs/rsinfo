Its highly porous nature and weak inter layer connections, makes diatomites fragile and brittle. Moreover, fracture toughness of diatomites is very low because of negligible grain interlocking or cementation (Barenblatt et al 2002). But, diatomite’s brittleness and low fracture strength is the key to flow of oil and gas in a rock type that is practically impermeable.
