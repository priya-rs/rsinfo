Necessary data that describe reservoir type and energy are essential to determine the best way for depleting the reservoir and achieving the ultimate recovery factor. Table (1) presents the different data necessary for reservoir modeling and reservoir simulation approaches. The data quality and quality control are crucial especially for studies concerned with the volumetric STOIIP validation. Data uncertainty is the most real term in oil industry (Diego T., et. al, 2016).
