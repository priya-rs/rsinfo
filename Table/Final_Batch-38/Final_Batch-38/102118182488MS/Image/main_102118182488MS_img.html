The Jansz-Io field is one of two super-giant gas fields delivering production for the Gorgon project (Figure 1 – Base map). Flawless start-up of the upstream system for Jansz-Io field was achieved in late 2015. This included 10 wells proved, successful start-up fluid management, upstream system start-up, delivery of fuel gas and production for the first LNG cargo.
