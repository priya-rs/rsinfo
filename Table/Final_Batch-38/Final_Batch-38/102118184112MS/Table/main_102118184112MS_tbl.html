Table 2 shows the predicted concentrations. The MOC sensor was not as sensitive to the variations among the SARA composition as the laboratory analysis. The sensor performance can be improved by characterizing additional oils of similar SARA composition to that of Well B, Well C, and Well D. At the time of the simulation, less than 2% of the samples in the optical database were heavy oils of similar composition.
