Barreto and Schiozer (2015) propose a methodology to optimize ICV placement design with fewer simulations than other traditional mathematical methods, using indicators to evaluate reservoir performance and guide the strategy construction. It adopts three types of rules (eliminatory, classificatory and design) to evaluate the field, well and well-monitoring regions (WMR). Table 1 shows the eliminatory rules to evaluate the potential of a region to implement an ICV.
