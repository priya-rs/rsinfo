Stage 5 of well #46 is selected to demonstrate the workflow for leakoff calculation. Same as all other stages, this stage has five clusters with average spacing of 60 ft (geometrical spacing). The parameters used for the leakoff coefficient calculation are listed in Table 2. Experimental data shows, according to Kamenov, Zhu, Hill, and Zhang (2013), the aperture of induced NF is an order of 250 μm, and its permeability could be 3.6 Darcy at the closure stress of 4,000 psi. With optical profilometer, Wu and Sharma (2017) measured that a fracture roughness is in the range from 5 μm to 250 μm (RMS). Thus, we carried out a sensitivity study for various roughness value.
