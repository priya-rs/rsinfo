With the help of MWD technology and Sofoil’s PolyGon software algorithms, a bottomhole pressure curve was obtained in hypothetical operation of a well in the absence of an environment when solving the multi-well deconvolution equation system (Fig. 14a).

When solving the multi-well deconvolution equation system, it is possible to obtain cross-well transient characteristics. Interpretation of these transient characteristics using the interference test algorithm allows obtaining a transmissibility in the interval between the well under investigation and affecting it nearby well. Convolution of cross-bore transient characteristics makes it possible to obtain curves that characterize the amplitude of the influence of surrounding wells in given pressure units on the investigated one throughout its operation.
