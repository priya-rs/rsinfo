Well shut-in simulations were performed using the wellbore hydraulic models following the 5-year production conditions. A well restart scenario was also included to see if any well trajectory would lead to a restart failure. Fig. 38 shows the liquid holdup distribution in the wellbore after shutting down the wells for 1 day. It shows there are liquid slugs in the lateral sections of the toe-down, hold-toe-down, and oscillating trajectories. These liquid slugs will arrive at the surface separator and will be a challenge to handle after the well is restarted.
