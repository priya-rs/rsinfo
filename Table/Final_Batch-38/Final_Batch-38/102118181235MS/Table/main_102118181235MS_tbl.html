Table 3 summarizes the pilot well characteristics. The tight-gas Mesaverde was the producing formation. The Mesaverde is composed of interbedded, thinly-layered sands that can require many hydraulically fractured zones for processing (Adeyeye et al. 2013; Juranek et al. 2010; Matheney et al. 2013). A typical well in the field has several thousand feet of perforations and a deviated or “S”-shaped geometry.
