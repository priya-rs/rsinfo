Conduct engineering research in the conditions of permafrost due to the nature of the area requires further development and inclusion of additional studies, since the standard set of studies unable to identify potentially dangerous areas with a possible thawing in the process of drilling and exploitation. As a result of such interactions may form hopper sagging in testing parts of the holes (fig. 1), voids within the entire range of frozen rocks that leads to a rise in drilling operations, in addition, can lead to the loss of longitudinal stability of wells, and, as a consequence, to the accident.
