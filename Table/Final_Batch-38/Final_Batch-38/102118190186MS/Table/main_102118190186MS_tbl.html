From Table 5 we can see that both of WZ-OS surfactant and GM-50surfactant are effective in foaming and acceptable as clean-up additives. As a kind of non-Newtonian fluid, the foam is featured with shearing and thinning abilities, while its apparent viscosity in high-permeability formation is higher than that in low-permeability one. Therefore, foams may be used to plug highly permeable formation to with higher a swept volume and sweep efficiency. They are stable in water but break in oil, so they may be used to plug water productive formation and let oil be discharged. In addition, the foam is a kind of surfactant that washes up oil efficiently, transferring to the top of the oil reservoir to foam a gas cap that helps drain remaining oil at the top.
