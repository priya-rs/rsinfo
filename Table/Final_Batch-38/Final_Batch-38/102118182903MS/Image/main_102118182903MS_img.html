The field under study is located approximately 280 km south east, onshore Abu Dhabi, UAE. The first two wells were drilled in 1990s and tested with oil and water acquifer in different reservoirs. The land surrounding this field is generally categorized as flat terrain with sand plains and dunes, Figure 1.

Well-03 was completed with dual completion string, Figure 8, (2-7/8″ & 3-1/2″) in order to maximize the economic value of the well and to avail the chance of testing and producing two different reservoirs in same well at same time. The integrity of the well was really a challenge in this planned long term production and injection operation. In order to evaluate the well for its integrity, a stress check analysis was performed to ensure the forces applied on packer are within the limit of provided equipment pressure rating.

A 2-3 days short production and injection cycle was completed successfully. Gauges were recovered from both strings and data was down loaded and analyzed for quality check, noise level and any communication between strings, which revealed successful results, Figure 9.

Gauges were placed in both string s to evaluate the interference during the simultaneous production and injection period. There was a 22 hours period of production from the long string; the pressure profile in the short string was recorded at the same time during the injection and fall of period. There was no evidence found for interference or abnormal response in the pressure profile of the observation string, (Fig. 10).

The 22 hrs of production from the long string was followed by 4.5 hrs of pressure build up while injecting in the short string. There was no indication of interference observed in the downhole pressure data (Production + PBU) from the long string during the injection period trough the short string, Figure 11.
