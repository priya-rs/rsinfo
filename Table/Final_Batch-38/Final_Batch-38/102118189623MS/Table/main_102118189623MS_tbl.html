Clean up flow. If flow is still via the 16/64th fixed choke, bean-up well in 4/64th choke increments every hour to achieve target rate. >= 770 5 (estimated)  
If slop tank capacity is unmanageable at any point, bean back by diverting flow through previous choke opening and manipulate chokes as desired until the clean-up criteria below is achieved. Manipulation between adjustable and fixed chokes is at the discretion of the CSV
Stop glycol injection when flowing tubing head temperature (FTHT) reaches 20 deg C. If 20 deg C is not achieved, continue to pump Glycol
Clean up criteria:
1) BSW stabilised at <=1% for at least 1 hour
2) Separator oil rate stabilized at +/- 200 Sm3/day for at least 1 hour
3) FBHP stabilised at +/-2 bar for at least 1 hour
4) FWHT stabilised at+/-2 deg C for at least 1 hour
End of Clean up: after clean-up criteria is achieved, shut-in well at choke manifold and subsequently at SSSV. Shut-in 6 (minimum)
