For Yaraktinskoe Filed an average time delay in injection system start is 1.5 year. Therefore the further stage of optimization program was to determine the production wells regimes during depletion period. To solve the problem the synthetic simulation model (“layer-cake” type) was constructed. (figure 3.10). Lithology and porosity distribution was performed in reliance on vertical proportion curves from wellbores in oil rims zones. Permeability and saturation were calculated in agreement to core correlations. Dimension and parameters of the model presented in Table A.1.
