After shutting in Y-24P, the injection rate declined steadily due to the increasing reservoir pressure. Two weeks later, the well Y-24P was re-opened to produce for several hours and showed a significant increase in performance with the oil rate reaching 1,500 stbopd and a significantly lower water cut of 15.7%. The well even reached the highest oil rate of 4,500 bopd one week after reopening. Following the re-opening of Y-24P, the injection rate in -12P declined gradually until less than 1.0 MMscf/d when it was decided to stop the injection. In summary, gas was injected for 54 days (as shown in Table 3), and the cumulative gas injection volume was 140 MMscf.
