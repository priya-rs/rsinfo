A formation tester with a simple circular probe was used to perform the miniDST because of the large mobility expected in the reservoir. Based on the logs, four points were selected for fluid pumpout; from top, the first point was at irreducible water saturation; second and third points were in the transition zone; and the last point was in the free water zone. Table 1 summarizes the wireline-conveyed formation tester pumpout points along with their purpose and associated water saturation. Fig. 5 shows the pumpout points along with formation evaluation log data and interpretation.
