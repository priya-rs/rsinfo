While it is often suggested that adjusting Eaton exponents to higher values will yield a match between log-based estimates and the observed overpressure in areas of suspected multiple overpressure mechanisms, however, the geological validity of changing the Eaton exponent in the context of pore pressure prediction and understanding the causes of overpressure is still diffuse.
