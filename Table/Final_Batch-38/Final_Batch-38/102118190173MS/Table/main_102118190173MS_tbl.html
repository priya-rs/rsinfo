It evaluated eleven schemes of steam + nitrogen injection using numerical simulation. In all schemes, it was tested remained constant volume of injected steam (except 2, corresponding to nitrogen injection only), while the volume of nitrogen varied according to the scheme (pre-injection, post-injection, co-injection or a combination of the above). Table 1 presents all the cases evaluated, red cases were discarding because according to the simulation results in Figure 7, their production is lower than the the base case (only steam). In all cases, it assumed nitrogen injection rate according to real drive capacity 1200 m3/hour (1017072 ft3/day).
