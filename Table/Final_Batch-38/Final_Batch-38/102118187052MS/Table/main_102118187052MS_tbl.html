The 26-in. hole sections are generally short and usually drilled in less than a day. However, there is substantial cost savings if suitable technology is applied for improving the performance and addressing the required technical challenges. Table 2 compares the time saved while batch drilling three 26-in. sections of the three target wells against the best offset ROP achieved while drilling with a PDC bit.
