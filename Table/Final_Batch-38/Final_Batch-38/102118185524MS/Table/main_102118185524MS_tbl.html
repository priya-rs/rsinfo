DGXJ was developed with water flooding in 1982. By the end of 2011, it witnessed comprehensive water cut up to 96.97%, and recovery degree of 40.82%, which showed that the oilfield was at a late- developed stage with -ultra high water cut and recovery degree”. This oilfield also witnessed multilayers with high interlayer heterogenity, developed water flowing channels and irregular and imperfect I/P well patterns, therefore it was imperative to find an effective IOR/EOR technical method. With the help of further comprehensive application of PI decision technology and Fuzzy Comprehensive Judgment Meth, the advantage flowing channel between each I/P well was identified and classified. On the basis of relevant physical modeling and numerical modeling (No details presented in this paper due to the limitation of word number), a new polymer assembly slug was designed. It was injected through Preset/Middle + Main Slug as shown in Table 2 with accumulated injection amount of 0.1PV (96.97*104 m3), and daily injection rate of 2470 m3 for 18 months.
