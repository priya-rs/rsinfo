In this scenario, the produced fluid GOR and water cut are functions of well online time. The boundary conditions used in the simulation are shown in Table 5. Water cut is a very high at the beginning and then gradually decreases. This represents the initial flowback period followed by stable 3-month production. GOR of the well is increasing with time. Note that the reservoir pressures in scenario 2 are 1,000 psia higher than those in scenario 1.
