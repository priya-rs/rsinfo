Reservoir pressure was obtained by well testing in nearby wells of well pad #10 and well 47R, subject to pressure drop by the well drilling time. Water cut was taken as a mean value of actual well performance in oil and water zone, that is, 10%. Well test data show permeability range to be 0.0005-173 mD. The calculation looks to 1.9 mD value, that is equal to mean permeability value for wells drilled within well pad #10 in 2015. The calculation results are shown in Table 2 and Fig. 4.
