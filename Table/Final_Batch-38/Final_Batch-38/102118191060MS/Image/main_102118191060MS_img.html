According to Offshore Drilling Manual, a well of the bottom hole temperature higher than 150°C and the BHP greater than 68.9 MPa or the formation pore pressure gradient exceed 1.80 g/cm3 is called the HTHP well [15]. The global HTHP oil and gas resources (shown in Fig.1) are widely distributed. With the reduction of conventional oil and gas, the HTHP will become one of the important areas for exploration and development. In recent years, there are about 27% major oil and gas discoveries from the HTHP field.

The South China YQ basin is one of the three major offshore HTHP regions in the world (shown in Fig.2). The basin is located at the intersection of the Eurasian, Pacific and India – Australia plates and has a complex geological structure. The bottomhole temperature (BHT) is 249°C and the BHP is 142MPa (pore pressure gradient is 2.4MPa/100m). Consequently, the basin is typical ultra-HTHP. The entire circulation process of the drilling fluid in the well can be regarded as a heat exchanger with certain boundary conditions [20-22]. The formation exchanges heat with the drilling fluid in the annulus which exchanges heat with the drilling fluid in the string. The entire cycle area is divided into five areas (shown in Fig.4). The first area is formation. The second area is annulus. The third area is drillstring. The fourth is the area inside the drillstring. The fifth is the area of bit.

The following is the process of equipment installation (shown in Fig.5).

Firstly, complete the wellbore dynamic pressure test (shown in Fig.6) to control BHP accurately. The ECD is dynamically increased to comply the test by adjusting the opening of the throttle valve to control the SBP and the fluctuations of BHP are within 1 psi.

Secondly, the SBP is applied during the tripping out to avoid the kicks caused by swabbing pressure (shown in Fig.7). In addition, the time of tripping out is saved to reduce the risk of sticking during pipe connection. The SBP is controlled to 120psi during the tripping out which is equivalent to increase 0.02sg of the ECD.

Firstly, the casing structure is optimized from 7-8 layers to 5 layers (shown in Fig.8).

Secondly, the drilling operation speed has greatly increased. Take the X-1 well for example, the planned time is 90 days and the actual is 63.63 days saving 26.37days (shown in Fig.9).
