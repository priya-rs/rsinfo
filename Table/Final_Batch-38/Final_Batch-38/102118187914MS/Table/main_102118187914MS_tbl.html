In this project, the emphasis is put on Ball Dropper technology (automatic ball launcher). The technology is designed for a safe multistage hydraulic fracturing under surface high-pressure injection. The traditional method suggests separate fracturing operations on a consequent stages to set the ball manually on surface. The ball launcher also made it possible to inject stages without intermediate mixture squeezing or breaks and, thus, to significantly reduce fluid volume in each port. Table 3 compares main parameters between two well groups - with the conventional multistage hydraulic fracturing technology and performed without breaks or squeezing – Ball Dropper.
