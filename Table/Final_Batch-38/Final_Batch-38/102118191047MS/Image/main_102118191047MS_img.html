Managed pressure drilling(MPD) is a widely used advanced drilling technology, which can effectively solve many drilling problems related to borehole pressure, such as circulation leakage, serious gas kick and narrow density window. Although MPD technology is very powerful, its application is based on the existence of certain positive security density windows. If there are negative density windows in the strata,, which occurs leakage in the upper section and collapse in the lower section. As shown in Figure.1. The conventional MPD technology will become powerless. This paper presents a new Gas-Lift MPD technology based on Dual-Channel Drillpipe, which can effectively solve the problem of negative density window.

Gas-Lift MPD drilling is a new technology that uses a small number of Dual-Channel Drillpipe to lift up the annular space drilling fluid in the upper well and reduce the ECD to drill though the leaky strata. Figure 2 shows how it works. It is developed from aerated underbalanced drilling, and the key is accuratly controlling of wellbore ECD profile.

Dual-channel Drillpipe (DCP) is one of the key equipment of Gas lift MPD technology. The DCP is composed of the 139.7mm DCP, the gas lift joint and the conventional 139.7mm drill pipe. It has two circulating channels shown in Figure 3. Channel 1 is for drilling fluid circulation and Channel 2 is directly connected with the upper borehole annulus.

Other main equipments include: gas liquid double injection special adapter, air compressor, Dual-Channel square Drillpipe, etc. as shown in figures 4 and 5.

Under the same pressure reduction, with the increase of well depth, the half parabolic profiles of ECD can appear in different depths of annulus,as shown in Figure 6. Under the principle that the Upper formation ECD is always less than the leakage pressure of the formation, the accurate continuous control of the annular ECD section is achieved by adjusting the flow of the injected gas, the depth of the double channel drill rod, the drilling fluid flow and the density of the drilling fluid. Keeping the Lower formation annulus ECD is always greater than its collapse pressure and improving its wellbore stability. Thus, the problem of negative density window drilling is solved. The Shun Bei oil field is located in the central and Western Tarim Basin and is located in the desert area of Gobi desert on the northern edge of the Taklimakan Desert. The surface is mainly desert and floating soil, and the elevation is usually between 950 and 990m. The regional structure is located in the Shun Tuo Guo Le low uplift tectonic zone, spanning Shun Tuo Guo Le low uplift and two depressions of the East and the West, the South adjacent to katalone and the northern part of the Sha Ya uplift. It includes 4 blocks: Shun Bei, Shun Tuo, Shun Xi and Ah Dong, covering an area of about 20 thousand square kilometers. As shown in Figure 8.

According to the characteristics of stratum and the need of exploration and development, the four levels well body structure is commonly used in the ShunBei oil field, as shown in Figure 9.

According to the analysis of completed well data, there are many fractures in the Permian igneous strata. The loss ECD is lower, and the requirement is less than 1.29g/cm3. However, the collapse ECD of the underpart Silurian hard brittle mudstone strata is higher than the 1.31g/cm3. In the course of drilling, if both of them are in the same open hole section, there is a negative density window of 0.03-0.05 g/cm3. It is difficult to use a single drilling fluid density system in conventional drilling. As shown in Figure 10:

In the early stage, pressure bearing plugging technology was used to improve the bearing capacity of Permian strata. The construction of 3 wells has been completed. The effect is shown in Figure 11.

It is very limited to improve the bearing capacity of the Permian strata by pressure plugging technology, and the maximum pressure ECD is about 1.32g/cm3, and the repeated leakage phenomenon is serious, reaching more than 80%.
