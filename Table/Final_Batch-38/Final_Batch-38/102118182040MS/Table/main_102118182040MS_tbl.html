The mapping results of pure oil thickness for each layer can be verified by new well logging. For instance, there are two wells named as Y151 and Y152 which were drilled in 2015. The interpretation results of pure oil thickness through new well logs are listed in table 2, including the calculation results of pure oil thickness generated by the saturation logs. As can be seen from the results, for most layers the results of the two methods are very close, which to some extent confirms that the result of remaining oil characterization is reliable. Only the results of Well Y151 in layer F and layer N are different, and so are the results of Well Y152's in layer L.
