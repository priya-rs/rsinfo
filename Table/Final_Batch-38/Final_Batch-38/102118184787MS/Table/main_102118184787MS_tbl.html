The x-ray diffraction analysis of five formation samples (2 from Botuobinskiy, 2 from Khamakinskiy, and 1 from Talakhskiy) found that total clay content is 10% or less. The main clays in Chayandinskoye are kaolinite, chlorite, and illite. The main components of the Botuobinskiy formation are dolomite and anhydrite (53 and 44% respectively), usually very sensitive to water and therefore increasing the risk of water blocks. The Khamakinskiy formation mainly consists of quartz (Table 1).
