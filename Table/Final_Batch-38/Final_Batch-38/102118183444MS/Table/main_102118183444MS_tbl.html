Compared with the microseismic monitoring results, it is obvious that (1) the microseismic image of X003 reflects evident characteristics of micro fractures with relatively uniform fracturing location, consistent fracturing strength and mean fracture aperture smaller than 2mm; (2) X001 and X005 show similar characteristics of mixed fractures, that is, the rock failure is locally concentrated (large fractures) but widely distributed (micro fractures), and the mean fracture aperture is generally within 2mm~3mm; (3) the microseismic image of X007 indicates apparent characteristics of large fractures where most of the hydraulic fracturing events happen at one to two intersection planes with the horizontal well with mean fracture aperture over 3mm (Table 2).
