From the petrophysical evaluation, absolute porosities were obtained, along with permeabilities, Gamma Ray logs, Density and Sonic logs for 70_2 sand in the IDE-1 well. The zones of interest were: 1st zone (5775’-5800’) tvd, 2nd zone (5730’-5772’) tvd, 3rd zone (5675’-5715’) tvd. (Table 1, Figure 2). The input PVT information entered for simulation comes from background bottom sampling performed in the AP01-PH well at level of 70_2 reservoir. Pb = 2246 Lpcm, API = 25,3 Rs = 357 PCN/BN, Bo = 1,191 BY/BN, μO = 2,61 Cp and surface conditions 139 psi and 140 °F.
