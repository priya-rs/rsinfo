In a bottom water drive, the aquifer is present below the entire reservoir and water influx moves vertically upward into the oil zone. In an edge water drive, the aquifer is located on the flanks of the reservoir and the water moves upward along the reservoir dip, different aquifer geometry will give different energy and give different end result in terms the value of maximal recovery factor based on log data and depth structure analysis, for example the edge water aquifer in asri basin sand 34-X (Fig.1).

The 34-X divided into 2 area east and west, start produce in 1990 in east area, the initial pressure was 1,600 psi (Fig.2), and west area start produce in 2000 with initial pressure at 1,000 Psi, after 27 years of production the 34-X reservoir pressure decrease to 800 Psi (Fig.3) quite stable for long period of time. From material balance method, the most robust and easy to understood is pressure history matching method, the pressure match method employs an iterative procedure that uses the values of original oil-in-place, original gas-inplace, and W to calculate the reservoir pressure versus time.

The tracer data is very useful to confirm well connectivity and reservoir heterogenity, some reservoir model need to be updated in terms of permeability distribution (facies modeling), because breakthrough time from the tracer are way more long rather than estimation from the model. For example at IN field, breakthrough estimation from simulation are 372 days for well IN-X (Fig.8), in a reality tracer detected after 884 days (Fig.9).

Compartmentalised reservoir in Windri area divided into 5 sweep pattern (Fig.13) to make analysis more detail and accurate. Each of compartment have different characteristic, and it help us to control and make better understanding about water injection condition and pattern in Windri area. Group 1 located in most western of Windri area, in consist of WF-03 producer and WF-04 injector wells and both of them can be pairing one injector and one producer. Group 2 structurally higher than group 1, it consist of WN-02, WF-01 producer and WF-02, WF-06 injector.

After evaluating the productivity of the well, upzinging the ESP is an opportunity to increase the production fluid, therefore the ESP was change from Q10 with capacity range 600 – 1250 bfpd to Q20 with capacity range 1150 – 2050 bfpd. With both combination of increasing fluid rate and decreasing water cut, WW D-29 become successful pilot project. The water cut was decrease from 98% to 83% in the lowest water cut, then sustain in 92%WC (Fig.14), while oil increase fom 20 BOPD to 300 BOPD (Fig.15).
