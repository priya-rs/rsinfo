From phase behavior tests on BBW#20 field, with various SEMAR, show that 13 out of 15 formulas were able to form middle phase micro emulsion between oil phase and water phase. Then, by considering efficiency of the results, the best 9 of13 formulas were selected for further selection using the spontaneous imbibitions tests (Table 4).
