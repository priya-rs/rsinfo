Figure 5 shows the histograms of each attribute and its classes. The mean values of each class are compared in Table 1. All the numbers shown in the figure and table are normalized between 0 and 1 to remove the impact of different scales of input data on training. Two classes show clearly distinctive characteristics in that Class 1 producers are in better reservoir and have large hydraulic fractures. All distributions and means of Class 1 are higher than those of Class 0. Producers in superior reservoir quality area coupled with larger hydraulic fracture sizes tend to be better oil producers.
