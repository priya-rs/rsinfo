Over a period of several years approximately twenty new cutter variants were laboratory tested, however less than five passed the laboratory test requirements to provide the correct cutter index necessary for the application. Table 1, below provides a sample of those that performed well (TS1, TS2 and eventually TS4). The TS4 with improved thermal resistance and higher toughness was the result of several iterations to refine and optimize based upon the failure diagnostics reviewed. This cutter provided the optimum performance and is the type utilized in the case studies discussed in the following sections.
