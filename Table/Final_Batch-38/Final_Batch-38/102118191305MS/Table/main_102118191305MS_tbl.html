The full Norne field reservoir model is obtained from the Norne database provided by the Center for Integrated Operations at NTNU (http://www.ipt.ntnu.no/~norne), and it consists of four segments: C, D, E and G (see Figure 2). The dimension of the reservoir model is 46 × 112 × 22. Therefore, the total number of reservoir gridblocks is 113344, among which 44927 gridblocks are active. Table 1 summarizes the association of each reservoir layer (1 – 22) to one of the primary formations, and this information is utilized to construct a vertical localization scheme for distance-based localization (Lorentzen et al., 2018), which will be adopted in the current study.
