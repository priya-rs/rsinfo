After injection, each tracer will disperse into the liquid (for the liquid tracer) and vapour (for the vapour phase tracer) and travel through the reservoir within the respective "phase". The vapour tracer will initially travel slowly through the formation as it will "stop and start" depending on whether there is any vapour (related to steam quality) present within the reservoir. The producer wells will need regular sampling of the production stream (sampling frequency outlined in Table 1) to allow analysis of the tracer concentration over time.
