Table 2 PBD design with uncertainties and output

A response surface model was developed using an optimal design which provides lower average prediction variance across regions of experimentation. This design was specifically used due to its predictive strength. The algorithm picks points that minimises the integral of the predictive variance across the design space. Target GOR, KV/KH, Krg, Sor and Aquifer strength were selected as heavy hitters. The time dependent assessment of uncertainty factor impact on recovery enabled the ability to identified heavy hitters that plays a significant role at a given point in time during flow period. 34 compositional model was ran using optimal design algorithm. See Table 3 below. GRV was included in the development of the RSM so as to assess possible uncertainty space in reservoir structures as they varies with depth.
