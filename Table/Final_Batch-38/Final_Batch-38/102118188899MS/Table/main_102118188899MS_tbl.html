The assignment in the FDC is also an opportunity to get more experienced professionals to directly collaborate with Subject Matter Experts (SME) therefore build on their skills to eventually become an SME for a particular subject. This interaction between current and potential future SMEs is particularly critical to ensure high level capabilities are preserved within the company in the long term.
