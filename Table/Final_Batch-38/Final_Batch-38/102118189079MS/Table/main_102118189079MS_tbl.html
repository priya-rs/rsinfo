The input history-match parameters were tuned until an acceptable history-match was obtained with the base model. Table 2 shows the values of the input history-match parameters that gave acceptable history-match. Figures 8 and 9 show the history-match plots of GOR, Oil Rate, SBHP/FTHP and water cut for A4-1p and A4-2p, respectively. For A4-1p, good history-match was obtained for initial produced GOR, SBHP, FTHP and water cut. In the same vein, reasonable history-match was obtained for A4-2p with respect to the GOR, FTHP and water cut.

Then, the ranges and probabilistic distribution of the input parameters are determined from reviewing data from target and analog reservoirs. In this work, 17 input parameters were identified and their ranges were gotten from review of A4 reservoir and analog reservoir data. Table 3 shows the input parameters and their ranges. With respect to the OGOC, it was assumed that the two fault blocks have independent OGOCs and the OGOC values range from the HKO of each fault block to the crest of each fault block.
