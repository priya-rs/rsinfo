But with jet-pump, the operator was not able to give the required draw-down. It was decided to produce the well on sucker rod pump. The pilot for installation of hydraulic rod pump unit was conducted for this well. The well has packer completion with 7" casing and 3-1/2" tubing. SSD has been installed in the well for jet-pumping.
