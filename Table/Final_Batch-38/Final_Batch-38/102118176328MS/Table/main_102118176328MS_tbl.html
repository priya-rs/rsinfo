Shortly after Kerisi came on stream it reached a plateau of c. 50,000 BOPD for about a year before started to decline. The production came from eight (8) producers and one (1) injector. In 2011, two (2) additional wells were drilled to drain unswept oil from UGMW and UG1ACW. Table 1 lists all Kerisi wells from initial development until now. All eleven (11) Kerisi wells (four out of them are horizontal wells with long horizontal section) were optimally placed by utilizing seismic attributes and MWD/LWD technology.
