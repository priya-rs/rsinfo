The project average flow back time is not known. A fixed CSS flow back and soak time can be related to total producing days and cumulative steam injected to approximate the average injection rate and volume. Three base cases cycle times were constructed to consider three different flow back times as shown in Table 2. One case was selected for matching the calculated cumulative oil and water production versus time with the historical type well. The actual production is given in Appendix B. Once a match was obtained, the remaining cases were tested using similar reservoir and operating parameters. The match results are graphically shown in Figure 3.
