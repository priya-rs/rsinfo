The project is located about 2 miles south of the City of Orcutt, on the southern flank of the Orcutt Oil Field, on the Newlove lease in northwestern Santa Barbara County, California. See Figure 2. Pertinent logs and completion data are available from the California Department of Conservation, Division of Oil, Gas and Geothermal Resources (DOGGR) website.12 Project specific model input parameters include those listed in Table 1.The oil gravity is understood to be 15 °API and well spacing equals about 0.625 acres. A listing of the model input data is given in Appendix A.

The project average flow back time is not known. A fixed CSS flow back and soak time can be related to total producing days and cumulative steam injected to approximate the average injection rate and volume. Three base cases cycle times were constructed to consider three different flow back times as shown in Table 2. One case was selected for matching the calculated cumulative oil and water production versus time with the historical type well. The actual production is given in Appendix B. Once a match was obtained, the remaining cases were tested using similar reservoir and operating parameters. The match results are graphically shown in Figure 3.

Insignificant differences are seen between all three base cases and the historical type well except that the cycle-by-cycle terminal oil production rate was10 to 15 BOPD higher in later cycles than the value of +/- 20 BOPD seen in the field. The calculated oil and water production by cycle plotted against time for Base Case 3 is shown in Figure 4. Given the high rate of decline for each cycle, it was estimated the terminal oil rate would be reached in couple of days and the match was deemed acceptable.
