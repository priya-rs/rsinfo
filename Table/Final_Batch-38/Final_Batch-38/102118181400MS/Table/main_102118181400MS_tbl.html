Analysis of measurements made during horizontal drilling operations were performed to provide additional support for the reservoir characteristics estimated from the production history matching. During horizontal drilling of the three wellbores, several parameters were measured with one foot resolution. These parameters can be used as relative indicators to estimate the production potential of the formation where the lateral was drilled. Average gas wetness ratios (GWR) and light-heavy ratios (LHR) were calculated as shown in Table 9.
