Model Verification. To verify the proposed well-testing model, we perform a model verification to compare our results with the numerical solutions for a MFHW in a composite reservoir, as shown in Fig.A1. The MFHW has five uniform and evenly-distributed hydraulic fractures, with a length of 30 m. The reservoir size is 2500 m × 2500 m × 10 m, modeled by hybrid grids (Fig. A1). The boundary sections can be arbitrary-shaped. Conveniently, they are described as hexagons. Detailed input information about the reservoir, fluid, and well is given by Table A1.
