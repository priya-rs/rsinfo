Pure CO2 is not miscible in Yates field due to the low average reservoir pressure (689 psi @ +1200 TVDSS). The HCM pilot utilized pure Y-grade NGL's, with the composition shown in Table 1, which is miscible with Yates oil at the current reservoir pressure and temperature. The goal of the pilot was to confirm that a miscible fluid can mobilize and recover remaining oil in Yates field. The cost of Y-grade NGL varies to a great extent proportionally to oil price and is subject to varying market conditions.
