Sandstone Group is subdivided into Lower Sandstone (predominantly sandstone), Middle Sandstone (claystone) and Upper Sandstone. Intrusives and volcanics further complicate the clastic sequence of the reservoir. The Upper Sandstone is characterized as a low permeable reservoir at which the permeability ranges from 0.1 to 20 mD. The reservoir contains a light oil of about 42 °API gravity and 0.38 cp of viscosity at initial reservoir conditions. A summary of the reservoir fluid properties is illustrated in Table 1.
