In late 1986, the Central Gas Facility (CGF) began operation, enabling the first field scale EOR project, Prudhoe Bay Miscible Gas Project (PBMGP), to start in early 1987. Since then, the PBMGP has expanded to more than 170 injection patterns in the main field of Prudhoe Bay. Miscible gas injection was expanded to Prudhoe Bay Eileen West End (EWE) in 2002 and to the satellite fields in 2003. The dynamic production allocation factor changes monthly as a function of the MI injection rates of the offset injectors. The MI rates which are relevant in this calculation are not current rates, but rather those from a prior month, determined by the transit time of MI from injector to producer.
