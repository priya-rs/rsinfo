In late 1986, the Central Gas Facility (CGF) began operation, enabling the first field scale EOR project, Prudhoe Bay Miscible Gas Project (PBMGP), to start in early 1987. Since then, the PBMGP has expanded to more than 170 injection patterns in the main field of Prudhoe Bay. Miscible gas injection was expanded to Prudhoe Bay Eileen West End (EWE) in 2002 and to the satellite fields in 2003.
