The well was logged after 72 hour's shut-in to acquire stabilized temperature. The tools were run in Long String under three conditions: Shut-in (both strings), injecting in the Short String only, and Transient (time-elapsed under shut in) conditions. A summary is shown in the Table below and in Fig.4. HPT was logged while RIH at 16.0 ft./min and the SNL was logged during POOH with stations every 3 feet.
