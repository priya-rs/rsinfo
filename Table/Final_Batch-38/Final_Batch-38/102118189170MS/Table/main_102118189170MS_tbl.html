The principal data used for this study was provided by Chevron Nigeria Limited. It includes well data (logs, mud weights, casing points, modular dynamic tester (MDT) and leak off test data) and Formation Tops. Twenty three wells were provided for the study out of which only four wells had the necessary data for this study (Table 1). Thesewells were picked based on proximity to the area of interest and depth of penetration. All the wells had gamma ray, resistivity, sonic and density logs.
