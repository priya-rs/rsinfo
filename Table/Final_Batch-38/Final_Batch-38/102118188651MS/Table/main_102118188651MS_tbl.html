Taking into account the distribution of wells available, different operating options can be accomplished, each one with several advantages and disadvantages (number of experiments repetitions, symmetry of operation, cost, etc.). After evaluating the different alternatives, the concept selected is: “Only one well pad is operated, O-05-02, with all the wells completed with pre-insulated tubing”. This option saves CAPEX and OPEX, and reduces the time needed to get representative results. Table 3 shows the proposed experiments, absolute steam flowrates, and number of wells to be applied.
