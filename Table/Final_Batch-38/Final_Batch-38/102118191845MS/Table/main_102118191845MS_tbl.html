Less than 30 minutes after mixing the initial samples, results of the emulstion test showed a clean resolution at the interface between all surfactant solutions and the produced crude oil tested (Montney or East Duvernay). No incompatibilities or precipitations were observed in this analysis. Table 1 summarizes the recorded surface tension values above the critical micelle concentration for the various surfactants tested in this study. MFS exhibited the lowest surface tension (29 mN/m) while NE showed the poorest (39 mN/m).
