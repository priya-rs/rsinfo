The amount of casing used is determined from the pore pressure gradient and fracture, which is the conventional method used by PDVSA, resulting a configuration of two casing. Once determine this configuration, proceeded the analysis of stresses applied to them, with the use of specialized software for this, which were considered the worst operating conditions which may occur in the hole depending on the group of efforts studied (burst, collapse and tension). Safety factors set out by PDVSA were added to the maximum stresses resulting in the pipe11. Through these steps is obtained the design features.
