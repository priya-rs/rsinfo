In diatomites, during the production and injection time, changes in pore pressure have caused them to crack and collapse because of poor inter-layer connection and inelasticity. Weak inter-layer connectivity and unpredictable elasticity cause a large range of Young's moduli and significantly varying Poisson's ratio in diatomites.
