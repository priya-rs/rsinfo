An objective function was defined to maximize both oil recovery and CO2 storage. A total of 23 possible uncertain variables were initially selected and assisgned upper and lower bounds as shown in Table 1. Sensitivity analysis with a Latin-hypercube sampling technique was conducted to study the uncertain variables’ effects on the objective function. Each variable was sampled three times totaling 69 simulations. The effect of each variable on the objective function was ranked. This procedure was utilized to reduce the number of control variables to 12 and to ensure the optimum solution with fewer simulation runs.
