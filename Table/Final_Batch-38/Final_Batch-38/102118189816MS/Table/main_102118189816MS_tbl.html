IOR techniques are divided into two basic categories: (1) pressure support methods like waterflooding and immiscible gas injection, and (2) enhanced oil recovery (EOR) techniques where rock and/or fluid properties are altered by the injectant. For the EOR techniques, there are three main classifications: miscible gas injection, chemical floods and thermal methods. Each classification can be further subdivided into individual methods.
